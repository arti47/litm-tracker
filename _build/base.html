Promise

Mark Promise when you evolve or replace a theme (and when trading theme parts). Fill all 5 → a Moment of Fulfillment: reset, carry over extra marks, and gain a Quintessence.

🎒 Backpack

Temporary story tags — packed items, prepared songs, memorised incantations. You start with one. Tap 🟡/🟠 to flip helpful/hindering; ✓ to scratch (single-use removed).

📓 Notes

🤝 Fellowship Theme

A shared fifth theme any Hero can invoke. Power tags are single-use — scratched when invoked, recovered while camping.

💞 Fellowship Relationships

A single-use story tag you hold for each other Hero. Renewed or rephrased around the campfire.

🟢 Statuses

Tier 1 (mild) → 6 (deadly/transformative). Tap a box to set the current tier; stacking pushes to the next free box. Your Limit is 5 — at tier 5 you're overcome, at 6 killed or transformed.

🟡 Scene Story Tags

Temporary tags in the scene/environment (vantage point, heavy rain, makeshift spear). Toggle helpful/hindering; scratch to remove.

🎲 Roll an Action

Tap a tag to count it: once = helpful (+1), twice = hindering (−1), again = off. Tap 🔥 on a helpful tag to burn it (+3 instead of +1, scratches the tag). Statuses: only your single highest helpful and highest hindering count.

Hero Development (per theme)

Improvemark when a weakness tag is invoked or you Reflect in camp. 3 → reset & gain an improvement (usually a new power tag).
Abandonmark when you ignore/betray the Quest. 3 → the theme is replaced (mark Promise).
Milestonemark when you achieve the Quest. 3 → the theme evolves (mark Promise).

Camping

Narrator expires story tags → establish the place → each Hero takes 2 activities (3 with Consequences): Rest (recover statuses & scratched power tags), Reflect (mark Improve on a theme), Camp Action (count Power, spend half without rolling, or roll). Then each Hero recovers a Fellowship power tag or renews one relationship tag.
