Promise

Mark Promise when you evolve or replace a theme (and when trading theme parts). Fill all 5 → a Moment of Fulfillment: reset, carry over extra marks, and gain a Quintessence.

🎒 Backpack

Temporary story tags — packed items, prepared songs, memorised incantations. You start with one. Tap 🟡/🟠 to flip helpful/hindering; ✓ to scratch (single-use removed).

📓 Notes

🤝 Fellowship Theme

A shared fifth theme any Hero can invoke. Power tags are single-use — scratched when invoked, recovered while camping.

💞 Fellowship Relationships

A single-use story tag you hold for each other Hero. Renewed or rephrased around the campfire.

🎬 Scene

The game loop. Tap the beat you're in; jot the stakes and what's looming.

🟢 Statuses

Tier 1 (mild) → 6 (deadly/transformative). Tap a box to set the current tier; stacking pushes to the next free box. A Hero's Limit is 5 (overcome at 5, killed/transformed at 6); lower the per-status Limit when tracking a Challenge or foe that's taken out sooner.

🎯 Progress & Limits

Track a multi-step goal toward a Limit — a project's making-progress, a chase's catch/outrun, unlocking a door, a breakthrough. Tap a box to set progress; fill to the Limit to complete it. The roller's Advance effect fills these.

🟡 Scene Story Tags

Temporary tags in the scene/environment (vantage point, heavy rain, makeshift spear). Toggle helpful/hindering; scratch to remove.

🎲 Roll an Action

Tap a tag to count it: once = helpful (+1), twice = hindering (−1), again = off. Tap 🔥 on a helpful tag to burn it (+3 instead of +1, scratches the tag). Statuses: only your single highest helpful and highest hindering count.

Might · Favored & Imperiled

Themes come in three Mights: Origin (ordinary) → Adventure (heroic) → Greatness (legendary). When you act, compare the Might the task demands to the Might you bring (your relevant theme/tag).

Matchedtask is about your level — no Might modifier (0).
Favored +3the task is below your Might — you outclass it.
Extr. Favored +6the task is far below your Might.
Imperiled −3the task is above your Might — out of your depth.
Extr. Imperiled −6the task is far above your Might.
Whereset this with the Might control on the Roll tab; the Narrator calls it.

Roll 2d6 + Power

10+Success — spend your Power on Effects. No Consequences.
7–9Success & Consequences — spend Power, but take a hit too.
6−Consequences — no Success.
⚅⚅Double sixes = Success without Consequences, any Power.
⚀⚀Double ones = Consequences without Success, any Power.
Min 1On a Success you always have at least 1 Power to spend.
PushOn 10+ you may accept Consequences for +1 Power.

Spending Power on Effects

After a Success, the Roll tab's Effects panel applies these for you. Worked examples live in the 📜 Action Grimoire.

Hero Development (per theme)

Improvemark when a weakness tag is invoked or you Reflect in camp. 3 → reset & gain an improvement (a power tag or Special Improvement).
Abandonmark when you ignore/betray the Quest. 3 → the theme is replaced (mark Promise).
Milestonemark when you achieve the Quest. 3 → the theme evolves (mark Promise).

Camping

Expire story tags → establish the place → each Hero takes 2 activities (3 with Consequences): Rest (recover statuses & scratched power tags), Reflect (mark Improve on a theme), Camp Action (count Power, spend half without rolling, or roll). Then each Hero recovers a Fellowship power tag or renews one relationship tag.
